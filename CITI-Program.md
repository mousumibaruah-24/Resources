* Everyone must take the Social-Behavioral-Educational (SBE) course at [CITI program](https://www.citiprogram.org/)
  - Step-by-step instructions on accessing this training are available on [ISB's website](https://www.isb.edu/irb/submit-irb-application/CITI-tutorial)
  - Note: This is *compulsory* for all RAs, regardless of whether you are working on project that requires fieldwork or not.
  - You are not allowed to start work without having completed the course; if it's been more than two years since you last took the course, please sign up for a refresher training.
